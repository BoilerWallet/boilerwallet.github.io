Can I send/receive ERC20 token xyz with Ballet?

Yes, all ERC20 tokens are compatible with Ballet.

* Can I switch to another Ethereum compatible network?

All Ethereum networks are compatible as long as you have at least one running node. (EIP155 is assumed to be implemented in the network)

Can I have multiple wallets?

Yes, you can create as many wallets as you wish and organize your assets accordingly.

Are my private keys stored in the cloud?

No, all private keys are stored only on your device. You are the only person with access to your private keys.

Are my private keys encrypted?

All your private keys are encrypted with a password you choose. Encryption is mandatory, but you can change your password at any time.

How are my private keys encrypted?

We are using the keystore specification from the Ethereum core developers to encrypt all your private key files individually. Behind the scenes the encryption algorithm AES is used, which was battle tested throughout decades and doesn't have any known vulnerabilities. The key for the encryption will be extracted from your password with a strong key derivation function named scrypt.

How do I know you are not a scammer?

All our repositories are open sourced on Github (Check out our Github page). We will not make any profits with those apps. All our implementations can be audited at any time by anyone.
